1. What are different ways to style your form??

Different Ways to Style a Form: Padding & Margins: Add space inside and outside form elements to improve readability. Borders & Backgrounds: Use borders to define the form area, and backgrounds to make the form stand out. Font Styling: Adjust font sizes, colors, and families to make the form visually appealing. Input & Button Styling: Style input fields and buttons to match the design, such as rounded corners or changing colors on hover. Label Positioning: Align labels next to or above input fields for clarity. Responsive Design: Use media queries to make the form look good on any screen size.
