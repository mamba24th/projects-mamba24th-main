What is the deifference betwwen OL and UL Tags

Ol or ordered list creates a list where items are numbered sequentially (1,2,3, etc.) It's used when the order of the items is important

Ul or unordered list creates a list with bullet poits (*) for each item. The order of items doesn't matter
