How to Perform an Ollie:

The ollie is the building block for most tricks. It's performed by popping the tail of the board while dragging the front foot upward to lift the skateboard into the air.

Watch on YouTube (Braille Skateboarding)

Mastering a Kickflip:

The kickflip takes the ollie to the next level by flipping the board under your feet using your front foot while maintaining balance in mid-air.

Watch on YouTube (Skateboarding Made Simple)

Improving Your Manual:

The manual involves lifting the front wheels off the ground and balancing on the rear wheels while rolling. It's all about control and practice.

Watch on YouTube (Skate Hacks Channel)
