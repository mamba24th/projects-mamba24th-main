My Favorite Hobbies - Final Project Proposal

Title and Topic of the Site

Title: My Favorite Hobbies

Topic: A personal website showcasing my hobbies—playing video games, playing guitar, and skateboarding. The site will provide tips, resources, and my personal experiences.

Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this topic because these hobbies are a big part of my life, and I want to share what I enjoy with others. The website will help others learn about these hobbies and how to get started with them.

Content on the Pages

  • Home Page: Introduction to the website, highlighting the hobbies I enjoy. A friendly welcome message with photos or illustrations of video games, guitar, and skateboarding.
  • Page 1 – Playing Video Games: Information on my favorite games, gaming tips, and recommendations.
  • Page 2 – Playing Guitar: Basic guitar tips, how I got started, and resources for learning.
  • Page 3 – Skateboarding: Tips for beginners, skateboarding tricks, and my personal experience.
  • Page 4 – Hobby Resources (Table): A table listing useful resources (websites, books, equipment) for each hobby.

Form and Table Content

Form Fields: Name, email, favorite hobby, message.

Table Data: List of recommended resources (websites, books, tools) for each hobby.

Content Collection

The content will come from my personal experiences with these hobbies, and I will use free image sources like Unsplash and Pexels for photos. References will be from hobby-related blogs or websites that provide helpful tips.

References (at least 2)

  • "Guitar Tips for Beginners" – Music Blog
