All of the images are captured while viewing the movie Big Hero 6

Hiro Hamada, the youngest in the family, has a natural talent for robotics. Recently, he’s been working on a project at the SFIT Robotics Lab, where he continues to innovate and push boundaries in robotics.

Tadashi Hamada was a kind-hearted and brilliant student known for his dedication to helping others. He designed Baymax as a healthcare companion, hoping to make a difference in the world. His legacy continues to inspire Hiro and others at SFIT.

Aunt Cass, Hiro and Tadashi's supportive guardian, has been there for them through thick and thin. She owns and runs a café in San Fransokyo, providing a warm and loving home environment. Her kindness and support have been instrumental in shaping Hiro’s journey.
