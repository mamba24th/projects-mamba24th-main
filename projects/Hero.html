Favorite Hero - Iron Man (Tony Stark)

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a brilliant billionaire inventor and CEO of Stark Industries. He creates advanced technology, including his iconic Iron Man suit, which grants him superhuman abilities like flight, strength, and various weapon systems. Despite his playboy persona, Stark is a genius with a strong moral compass who fights to protect the world as a member of the Avengers. His intelligence, wit, and self-built armor make him a key superhero in the Marvel Universe.
